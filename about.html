Who We Are

Healing From the Earth, For You

Mamosana Wellness Boutique is a holistic wellness centre rooted in the heart of Tembisa, Gauteng. Founded with a deep belief in the healing power of nature, we are dedicated to making holistic health accessible, affordable, and effective for every family.

Our name, "Mamosana", reflects our core purpose – nurturing wellness and wholeness. We draw on both ancient African healing traditions and modern natural medicine to bring you a curated collection of the finest herbs, supplements, teas, sea moss, and natural oils.

Every product in our boutique is hand-selected for purity, potency and effectiveness. We don't believe in shortcuts. We believe in healing – deep, sustainable, whole-body healing.
