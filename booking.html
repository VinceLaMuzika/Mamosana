Frequently Asked Questions

How long is a session?

Most sessions are 45–60 minutes. Initial sessions may be longer as we conduct a thorough wellness assessment.

Is there a session fee?

Please contact us via WhatsApp or email for our current session fees. We offer affordable packages for all budgets.

Can I consult online/virtually?

Some of our advisory sessions are available virtually via WhatsApp video call. Hands-on treatments like Ionic Foot Detox, Access Bars and Reiki require an in-person visit.

What should I bring?

Any existing medical records or diagnoses if relevant, a list of current medications or supplements, and your wellness goals.
