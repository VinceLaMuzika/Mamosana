FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does delivery take?

Orders are dispatched within 1–2 business days. Pep Paxi typically takes 2–4 days. Courier Guy delivers door-to-door in 1–3 business days.

Can I return a product?

We accept returns on unopened products within 7 days of delivery. Please contact us via email or WhatsApp to initiate a return.

Do you ship outside SA?

Currently we ship within South Africa only. International shipping may be available in future. WhatsApp us for more information.

How does PayFlex work?

PayFlex lets you split your purchase into 4 equal instalments over 6 weeks – completely interest free. Simply select PayFlex at checkout.

Are your products 100% natural?

Absolutely. Every product we carry is 100% natural with no harmful additives, synthetic chemicals or fillers. Natural integrity is our core value.

How do I track my order?

After dispatch you'll receive tracking details via email and WhatsApp. You can also message us directly on WhatsApp for real-time updates.
